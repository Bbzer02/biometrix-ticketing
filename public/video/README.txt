Login welcome video
===================

To show a video next to the welcome text (technical support, solutions, digital operations):

1. Add an MP4 file named:  helpdesk.mp4
2. Place it in this folder:  public/video/helpdesk.mp4

The video will play automatically in the login card left section (muted, looped).
Use a short clip that fits the tagline (e.g. support desk, teamwork, digital tools).
Recommended: under 30 seconds, MP4 (H.264) for best compatibility.
